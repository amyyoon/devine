FROM FIELD TO GLASS SINCE 2007.

In 2007, John and Catherine Windsor grew de Vine from a strong love of wine,
community, and Vancouver Island. Along with a dedicated team of friends and family and 25 acres of overgrown farmland, a vineyard was planted.

WE ARE A TRUE SMALL-BATCH, CRAFT DISTILLERY CREATING HANDCRAFTED SPIRITS OF THE HIGHEST QUALITY FROM BC GROWN INGREDIENTS.
